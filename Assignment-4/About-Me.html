About Me

School

I am a fourth year Dramatic Arts student at Brock University, and I'm set to graduate in June 2020! I mainly focus in production courses because I'm hoping to work as a stage manager after graduation. I've already stage managed 2 full productions and 1 staged reading here at the school. You can see more of my work on my resume page (I'm available for hire starting May 2020!)

Interests

In the last couple of years I've been getting more into playing video games. I mainly play PC games but sometimes I'll change it up with some PS4, 3DS, and even some mobile games if I'm laying in bed looking for something to pass the time. I also really like dogs. A lot of my time at home is spent taking care of my dog, going on walks with her, or taking her to the dog park. After she's all tired out, she sits by me on the couch while I play video games.

Goals

My main goal after graduation is to be able to support myself through just stage management, which is a pretty lofty thing in itself. I'd also love to have a nice house with a big backyard and huge windows so I can always be close to the outdoors (but still be able to stay in when it's too cold outside). The thing about theatre is that it's not really a trusty way to pay the bills, so one of my other goals is to stick with programming and keep it fun for myself (and maybe I'll be able to make some money off it on the side).
